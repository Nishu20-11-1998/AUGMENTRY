COMPONENTS OF FXMIRROR

FXMirror reflects user's body dimension and generates 3D avatar in real time.
The personalized avatar reflects user's facial expressions and physical movements immediately
based on MR (Mixed Reality) technology and tracking technology.

COMPONENTS OF FXMIRROR THE REAL FITTING

Our system utilizes patented technology, virtually recreating the shopper's body
and skeleton based on the analysis of the heights, feature points, cloth DB implementation,
depth map data and shows the 3D garment fitting simulation in real-time.
